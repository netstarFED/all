工作流（nsWorkflowVo）

参数说明

英文名称 按钮文字 类型 工作流类型 备注
nsWorkflowViewer 流程监控 workflowViewer
nsWorkflowViewerById 流程监控 workflowViewerById
nsWorkflowSubmit 提交 workflowSubmit submit
nsWorkflowSubmitClose 提交并关闭 workflowSubmit submit isCloseWindow：true
nsWorkflowMultiSubmit 批量提交 workflowSubmit multiSubmit requestSource:'checkbox'
nsWorkflowReject 驳回 workflowSubmit reject
nsWorkflowCancelSign 取消签收 workflowSubmit cancelSign
nsWorkflowWithdraw 撤回 workflowSubmit withdraw
nsWorkflowRollback 回退 workflowSubmit rollback
nsWorkflowRebook 改签 workflowSubmit rebook
nsWorkflowHasten 催办 workflowSubmit hasten
nsWorkflowEmergency 应急 workflowSubmit emergency
nsWorkflowComplete 签收 workflowSubmit emergency
nsWorkflowForWard 提交 workflowSubmit forWard 先签收后提交
nsWorkflowSubmitAllBatch 提交 workflowSubmit submitAllBatch 批量提交批次
nsWorkflowFindHandleRec 查看办理意见 workflowSubmit findHandleRec
nsWorkflowUnduComplete 取消结束 workflowSubmit unduComplete

*  添加工作流按钮，添加代码时要添加 functionConfig:{defaultMode:'workflowSubmit'}，
  原因根据页面参数(已办/完结)判断非工作流按钮只读，所以如果是工作流按钮需要添加
  functionConfig:{defaultMode:'workflowSubmit'}
